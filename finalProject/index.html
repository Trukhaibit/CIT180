UK Countries and Flags

  • England

  • Scotland

  • Wales

England

England's Flag;
The Cross of St. George
St. George's Banner

St. George's flag is a cross on a white field and is included as part of the UK's flag, the Union Jack.

Scotland

Scotland's Flag;
St. Andrew's Cross
Saltire

St. Andrew's Cross is a white saltire on a dark blue field and is included as part of the UK's flag, the Union Jack.

Wales

Wales' Flag;
Y Ddraig Goch
(The Red Dragon)

The Red Dragon is a flag that is white on it's top half, green on it's bottom half, and features a red european dragon in the center, it is not represented in the Union Jack

Northern Ireland

N. Ireland's Unofficial Flag;
The Ulster Banner
Red Hand Flag

Although not a country, this region's previous flag, St. Patrick's Saltire, a red saltire on a white field, is included as part of the UK's flag, the Union Jack.
